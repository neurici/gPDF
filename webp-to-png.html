What is gPDF?

gPDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, gPDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our WEBP to PNG tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Convert as many images as you need.
  • Privacy First: All processing happens locally in your browser.
  • Open Source: Verify our privacy claims and contribute.
  • High-Quality Output: Crisp PNG images from your WEBPs.
  • No Registration Required: Start converting instantly.

How to use the WEBP to PNG tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag & drop or select your .webp files.
  2. Step 2: Click the "Process Files" button.
  3. Step 3: Download your PNG image(s) from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my WEBP images uploaded to a server?

No. Everything happens in your browser. We never upload or collect your files.

Will the quality be preserved?

We convert using high-quality canvases. You can expect excellent fidelity for most images.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Yes, batch conversion is supported. Add multiple files before processing.
