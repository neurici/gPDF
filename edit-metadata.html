What is gPDF?

gPDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, gPDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our Edit PDF Metadata tool?

  • Private by Design: 100% client-side — your files never leave your device.
  • Open Source & Free: No registration, no ads, unlimited use.
  • Batch Editing: Load multiple PDFs and apply consistent metadata quickly.
  • Precise Control: Edit title, author, subject, keywords, creator, producer, and dates.

How to use

  • Load your PDF(s): Add one or more PDFs using drag & drop or the file picker.
  • Review fields: Existing metadata is auto-filled from the first PDF. Edit as needed.
  • Apply: Click Apply Metadata to generate updated PDFs to download.

Privacy

Everything happens in your browser. Your documents never leave your device. gPDF is open-source and doesn’t collect file data — ever.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my PDFs uploaded to a server?

No. Processing happens entirely in your browser. We never upload or collect your files.

Which metadata fields can I edit?

Title, Author, Subject, Keywords, Creator, Producer, and Creation/Modification dates.

Will changing metadata alter the PDF content?

No. Only document properties are updated. Page content remains unchanged.
