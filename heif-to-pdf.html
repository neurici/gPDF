Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are HEIC and HEIF formats?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) and HEIF (High Efficiency Image Format) are Apple's modern image formats that provide better compression than JPEG while maintaining higher quality. HEIC is the container format while HEIF is the underlying format. They're the default formats for photos taken on newer iPhones and iPads.

Can I convert multiple HEIC/HEIF files into a single PDF?

Yes! You can choose to either convert each HEIC/HEIF file into its own separate PDF document, or combine all images into a single PDF with each image on its own page.

Will the image quality be preserved?

Yes, our tool maintains the original image quality during conversion. The HEIC/HEIF images are converted to high-quality format before being embedded in the PDF.

Can I use this tool on my iPhone or iPad?

Yes! This tool works on iOS devices. When selecting photos from your Photos app, the tool will automatically detect and convert HEIF/HEIC images. If you encounter any issues, try selecting photos directly from the Photos app rather than Files app.

Is it safe to convert my personal photos here?

Absolutely. The entire conversion process happens in your browser. Your photos are never sent to or stored on our servers, ensuring your personal images remain completely private and secure.
