What is gPDF?

gPDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, gPDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our SVG to PDF tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Convert as many SVG images to PDF as you need, without any cost or restrictions.
  • Privacy First: Your files are processed directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available, so you can verify our privacy claims and even contribute to the project.
  • High Quality: Images are embedded without unnecessary recompression for excellent fidelity.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without the need to create an account.

How to use the SVG to PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your .svg files into the upload area, or click to select them.
  2. Step 2: Choose whether to merge into a single PDF or create individual PDFs.
  3. Step 3: Click the "Process Files" button and download your results from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my SVG files uploaded to a server?

No. Processing happens entirely in your browser. We never upload or collect your files.

Will the quality be preserved?

Yes. SVGs are vector graphics and are embedded with excellent fidelity. There is no unnecessary recompression.

Can I convert multiple SVGs at once?

Yes. Add multiple files and choose to combine them into a single PDF or export individual PDFs.
