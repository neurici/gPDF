Our Story & Philosophy

As college students, we constantly faced the frustrating challenge of converting & modifying PDF files for homeworks, projects, and other files with sensitive information such as names, financial information and more. We quickly realized that existing PDF-WebApps just didn't cut it. They all demanded sign-ups, subscriptions for basic functions, were painfully slow, had clunky user interfaces, and, worst of all, compromised our privacy by allowing them access to our sensitive documents. The idea of sacrificing our privacy to corporate entities just to convert a file was unacceptable.

This frustration fueled the creation of gPDF: an Open-Source, Privacy-Focused Alternative to popular PDF WebApps. While others forced you to register, pay subscriptions, store your data in the cloud, and offer subpar user experiences, we've laser-focused on two core principles: Privacy and User Experience. So we got to work, developing a service that's not only incredibly lightweight and efficient but also completely free and privacy-focused. At gPDF, we never collect any data on the files you upload or modify. Your documents stay on your device, just as they should. A crazy concept other services can't grasp.

You might be wondering: "Why is gPDF free and unlimited? Why no registrations? How do you cover server costs? What is your incentive?" We believe in complete transparency about our philosophy and how we operate. gPDF is, and always will be, completely free, with no registration needed, and truly unlimited. Because we crafted gPDF to have all file conversions and modifications performed directly on your computer, client-side, your files never leave your device and we never touch, view, or store them. This client-side processing also has significant benefits for you: it allows us to maintain a lightweight, fast, and efficient codebase, offering a service that's faster, cheaper, and more secure than our competitors. We strongly believe that there is no other service out there that is as privacy-focused as gPDF while being completely free to use, unrestricted, with no registration needed.

Our plan for affording server and hosting costs is straightforward: sponsorships, and voluntary donations. No trackers & data harvesting ads. All we're asking simply is, if you value what we do, we warmly welcome your support, whether its just recommending our site to a friend or colleague, finding bugs, suggesting new features, or donating through BuyMeACoffee. Any donators/sponsors will have their names/banner and a custom message of their choice listed on the webapp, as a Thank You.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is gPDF For?

gPDF is built for anyone who needs to modify their PDF Files efficiently and with privacy in mind. We specifically cater to Students, Freelancers, Small Businesses, Tech Enthuasists, privacy-conscious indvidiuals, & people who just want a better PDF WebApp.

How does gPDF protect my privacy?

Your privacy is our top priority. All file processing is done directly in your browser (client-side). This means your files are never uploaded to our servers, and we never see or store your data. What you do with your documents remains your business alone.

Are there any limitations on file size or usage?

No, there are no limitations. You can use our tools as much as you want, with any file size that your browser can handle. We don't believe in restricting access to essential features. Note that although there no limitations to file size or usage, the performance of gPDF largely depends on your device's hardware. Depending on the operation, you can generally expect 1000 page PDFs to be modified almost instantaneously.

Why is gPDF Open-Sourced?

Open-sourcing gPDF is our way of ensuring transparency and trust. We want to build a small community of problem solvers, solving the problem to why PDF WebApps suck, in 2025. Our ultimate goal for gPDF is to just be the best PDF WebApp, and we can't do this alone. If you find any bugs, have suggestions, ideas, or want to contribute to the project in any way, please feel free to do so and email us: neurici@gmail.com.

How is gPDF funded?

gPDF is funded through sponsorships and voluntary donations from users who appreciate our service. We do not rely on ads or selling user data. If you find our tools useful, we welcome your support to help us cover server costs and continue development.
